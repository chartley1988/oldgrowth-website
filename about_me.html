Introduction

Hi! I'm Carson, aka Chartley. I'm a carpenter turned developer (hopefully). OldGrowth is a project made with Godot I started during the pandemic. The goal was to learn the basics of programming, while also learn many of the other skills needed to build a game entirely on my own. This website is made with a similar goal, but with the focus being on HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Hopefully you'll enjoy witnessing the results of the many lessons I'm sure to face as I work towards those goals!
